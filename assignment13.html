Assignment 13

In this assignment I learned that something as relatively simple as a canvas element can be used to make complex shapes and graphical compositions embedded in the web page itself. When I started this assignment, I was a little intimidated since I don’t have much design experience at all. After working through the activities and building out my canvas for this webpage, I feel much more confident about my abilities to use this design element. The most helpful thing for me while working through this assignment was just to remember that the canvas is essentially a grid, so drawing shapes comes down to mapping the correct points, curves, and lines on that grid much in the same way I’d map functions in algebra.
