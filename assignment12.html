The biggest takeaway from this lesson for me was with regards to the assumptions I made about web accessibility while working through all my assignments. I didn’t realize that having sectioned content specifically tagged in the HTML code would be so impactful. I hadn’t considered the impact but after updating some of my later assignments to be more accessible, they were a bit easier for me to go back and interact with as well.
